Why Library 21?

  • Highly Scalable Business Model: The Company has developed curriculum and learning materials which are universal and can be used across the globe, with a strong focus on providing knowledge which would be relevant in the upcoming technology revolution. With a presence in Eleven (11) countries across the globe, the Company has proven its ability to scale and replicate across geographies, which it intends to continue, with a focus on being present in over 15 countries by 2022
  • Low Marginal Costs will allow for sustainability of High Margins: The Company boasts 50% gross margins as marginal costs are low and quality content is provided by an affordable rate which helps cater to various socio-economic classes. Further scale will only help in increasing margins and sustainability
  • Product Acceptability Across Customer Types: Some of the worlds leading educational and corporate institutions are customers, who have recognized the value of the curriculum and learning platforms being offered by the Company. These include Saudi Aramco, HSBC, Barclays, Misk Foundation, Riverdale Country School, Tuscany School, AlMadar International School, Careem, AJK Government Summer of 2020 saw 100,000 enrollments with over six (6) million enrollments expected by Q2CY2021 and ten (10) million by end 2021
  • Institutional Shareholding: AlArabi is a MENA region venture firm which has invested in Ed-tech and Health-tech companies across the investing spectrum. Avalon Ventures is a 30 year old VC firm out of the Bay area and has funded over 100 companies around the world with vast experience in value creation across sectors through their seasoned team of entrepreneur partners. Fidelity backed 8 Eight Roads, with fifty years of investment experience and partnerships with over 250 companies and investment teams
  • Projects in Pipeline: TutorBooth 2.0 Commencing; AI integration and predictive learning; Regional expansions in UK, South Africa and Oman; Large scale government contracts being signed across Asia which include Careem (500k+ users), FoodPanda (over 100k+ users) as the company moves from B2C to a B2G & B2G model becoming an education operator as well. C-Level staff being added to strengthen management for future expansion
  • Business Model Arbitrage: Ability to move business models from where they are developed to where they are needed is at the heart of the competitive advantage, allowing first world innovation to be easily distributed in emerging economies
  • Rapid Adoption of Disruptive Technology: Awareness and adoption of new emerging and changing economies allows the Company to provide cutting edge offerings to its users. Having a global presence allows us to move technology from where its developed to where its needed
