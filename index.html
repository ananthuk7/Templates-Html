FAQs on Covid PCR Test

Do you do the PCR test at home?

Yes, we do have dedicated and experienced team for the PCR test at the comfort of your home.

How soon can the test be done?

6 to 8 hours is the minimum time required to obtain the result.

How can I get the test report?

The result will be sent to you through SMS and email, however if you don’t receive on proposed time frame, you may contact our customer service agent through WhatsApp and you will be getting the updates.

What will be the cost?

Normal test with 24 hours report (TAT) costs AED 250 per test.

After the report, can the test result be added to Al Hosn App?

The test result is integrated and will be updated to Al HOSN app automatically within one hour from the report release time and if you face any difficulty, you may contact Al Hosn App customer care or Our Customer care for more assistance.

Do you accept card payment?

Payment can be made by cash or bank transfer .Upon request we too have the facility to send you payment link through which you can make payment online using your Debit / Credit Card.

Can I bank transfer? Bank details please?

Yes, you can bank transfer the payment and upon request our customer care representative will forward you the bank details.

Is the test reliable and DHA approved?

Yes, we are DHA approved authorized SWAB collection facility for the PCR sample collection and test is conducted at DHA approved Lab which is CAP accredited As well as ISO certified.

How to check if my test result is authentic?

The QR code on the report top right corner can be scanned for the authenticity

From which lab do you get the test done?

The test is done at AGIOMIX lab.

Which currency does u accept?

We accept UAE Dirhams, Dollars and Euros

What should I do if am tested positive?

When you receive a positive result it indicates that you most likely have infected with COVID-19. Many of them have only minor infections and have got a potentiality to recover so safely at home without the need of much medical attention. And if you have worse symptoms or if you have any doubts or questions regarding your medical conditions you should seek the support of Medical provider for your support and guidance.

If I have come in contact with a positive patient can I get my test done for free?

No, it has to be paid

If am positive will I get refund?

Our service is a paid service

Where do I Quarantine?

You have to follow the DHA guidelines and to know more about guidelines you may contact DHA.

Will my sample be altered?

No, Sample is collected and transferred to the LAB as per the DHA guided protocols.

I did not get any SMS or email from your side about the sample collected confirmation.

You can connect with our team anytime and our customer support team there to assist you anytime.

What are your working hours for sample collection?

We are available from 9.00 am to 10.00 pm
